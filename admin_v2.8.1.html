① Paste device MACs
One MAC per line. Format EC:62:60:EF:3E:90 — colons, hyphens, spaces all fine. 12 hex chars total.
② Verify against device serial output
Each Holly prints its expected SSID + password at 115200 baud when the captive portal launches. Compare against the device's serial output. If they don't match, do not flash the rest.
③ Print labels
A4 portrait, 4-up grid. Stick on Holly's base. Match printed device ID to the label on each unit.
📋
Dispatch workflow
  1. Flash Holly with v5070, factory reset (hold button 20s) so portal launches.
  2. Open serial monitor at 115200 — watch for [portal] AP password: line.
  3. Copy MAC into the box above. Click Generate.
  4. Cross-check the password in the verify panel against what serial printed.
  5. Print, stick to Holly's base, ship.
